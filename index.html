Instructions

  • You begin as a spaceship in the bottom center of the screen.
  • As asteroids approach you must click on them to disintegrate them.
  • Don't let the asteroids touch the sides or you will lose!
  • You gain points with each destroyed asteroid.
  • Once XP bar is filled, the speed of the asteroids increase in the next round.
  • At the end of the game the users points get added to the leaderboard.
